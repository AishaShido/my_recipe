Pasta with seradine and tomato sauce

Shopping list

  1. Pasta
  2. Tomato sauce
  3. Onion
  4. Seradine 120 g
  5. Red pepper

Ingredients

  • 1 red onion
  • 2 red peppers
  • 120 g seradine
  • 1 can of (450g) tomatoes
  • 1 cup of water
  • olive oil
  • garlic
  • oregano
  • paprika
  • 50 gram of pasta per person

Method

  1. Cut the onion and red peeppers into small pieces
  2. Heat some olive oil in a pan and fry the onions and the seradine
  3. Add origano, paprika, garlic, tomato and water then cook them for about 20 minutes
  4. Cook the pasta in a big pot of boiling water
  5. Serve the pasta with the sauce and enjoy it!

Tips for writing

  1. When writing a recipe or instraction, use numbers to indicate the stages
    and use the base form of the verb (imperative) to give instruction.
  2. Use commas between things in a list. Use ' and' between the last two things.
